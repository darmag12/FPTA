Transit Fuels

The Florida Public Transportation Association (FPTA) is one of the nations leading state transit associations, representing 40 urban and rural transit systems, two commuter railroads, a heavy-rail system, and two people mover systems.

ECONOMIC IMPACT OF FLORIDAS PUBLIC TRANSPORTATION:

$1 invested in public transportation
generates $5 in economic returns.

$10 million in capital investment results in
$30 million in increased business sales.

$10 million in operating investment yields
$32 million in increased business sales.

Home values near public transportation are
up to 24% higher, and hotels with direct
rail access to airports earn 11% more revenue per room than cities without.
(Source: American Public Transportation Association, www.APTA.com)
